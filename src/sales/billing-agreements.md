title: Billing Agreements
---

The Billing Agreements grid lists all [billing agreements]({{ site.baseurl }}{% link payment/paypal-billing-agreements.md %}) between your store and its customers. The store administrator can filter the records by the customer or billing agreement information including billing agreement reference ID, status, and creation date. Each record includes general information about the billing agreement, and all sales orders that have used it as a payment method.
